Links and Buttons and Things that are Neither

All the following elements have a JS click event handler attached:

6 anchor href=# 7 anchor empty href 8 anchor no href 9 span 10 span tabindex=0

Notes

  • Only certain element types receive focus by default:
    • a(href), input, button, textarea, select.
  • An anchor tag with no href attribute is not a link.
  • Buttons respond to ENTER or SPACE key; Links, only ENTER.
  • When they do respond, they also spawn a click event.
  • Arbitrary elements such as span or div need help:
    • tabindex="0" to be focusable
    • role="button/link/etc" for accessibility semantics
    • mouse & keyboard event listeners for interaction
